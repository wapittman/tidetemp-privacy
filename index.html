TideTemp Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 27, 2026

Overview

TideTemp is committed to protecting your privacy. This app is designed to provide water temperature, tide, and related environmental information without collecting personal data.

Information Collection

TideTemp does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information.

  • No account or login is required
  • No personal data is collected
  • No tracking or analytics are used

Data Sources

TideTemp retrieves publicly available environmental data from external sources such as NOAA APIs. These sources provide environmental measurements only and do not include user data.

Device Information

The app does not access or collect sensitive device data. It only performs network requests to retrieve public data.

Third-Party Services

No personal information is shared with third parties. External services are used only to retrieve public environmental data.

Data Storage

Any app data (such as selected stations or preferences) is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted or linked to you.

Children’s Privacy

TideTemp does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

This policy may be updated. Changes will be reflected on this page.
