TideTemp Privacy Policy Last updated: May 27, 2026 Overview TideTemp is committed to protecting your privacy. This app is designed to provide water temperature, tide, and related environmental information without collecting personal data. Information Collection TideTemp does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information. No account or login is required No personal data (such as name, email, or location) is collected No tracking technologies or analytics frameworks are used Data Sources TideTemp retrieves publicly available environmental data from trusted external sources, including but not limited to: NOAA (National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration) APIs Other publicly available water and weather data services These data sources provide environmental measurements only (e.g., water level, temperature, wind conditions), not user data. Device Information TideTemp does not access or collect sensitive device data. Standard system services (such as network requests) may be used to retrieve data, but this does not involve collecting any personal information. Third-Party Services TideTemp uses external data providers solely to retrieve public environmental data. No personal information is shared with these services. If advertising or analytics features are added in the future, this policy will be updated accordingly. Data Storage TideTemp may store limited app data locally on your device (such as selected stations or preferences) to improve user experience. This data: Remains on your device Is not transmitted externally Is not linked to your identity Children’s Privacy TideTemp does not knowingly collect personal information from children of any age. The app is safe for general audiences. Changes to This Privacy Policy This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be reflected by revising the “Last updated” date above.
